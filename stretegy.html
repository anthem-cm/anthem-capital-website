Opportunity

In recent years, the investment world has largely shifted towards multi-strategy funds, with many firms focusing on high-Sharpe, low-volatility approaches, competing internally within one organization. However, global macro has made a strong comeback, as rising interest rates and economic volatility have created long/short opportunities not seen in decades. Our fund is strategically positioned to capture these trends, leveraging a combination of disciplined macro strategies and cutting-edge execution methodologies. By utilizing the same quantitative principles employed at major hedge funds, we aim to exploit these macro shifts with precision, maximizing returns in this unique environment.

Heartbeat

Heartbeat is our proprietary data science toolkit, built from over a decade of research and development. This robust web application consolidates advanced analytics, enabling us to design, backtest, and optimize trading strategies. It also serves as a strategy archive, alerting us when saved strategies become active in the markets we trade. We refer to this powerful toolkit as "Silo," ensuring real-time insights and precise execution.
